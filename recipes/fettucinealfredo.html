Fettucine Alfredo

Description

Fettucine alfredo is tender pasta in a luscious cream sauce with garlic and fresh parmesan cheese. A classic Italian-American favorite, alfredo pasta is easy to make at home with simple ingredients

ingredients

  • 10 ounces fettuccine pasta
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 5 chopped garlic cloves
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 cups freshly grated parmesan cheese
